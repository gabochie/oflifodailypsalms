Psalms for When You Need Protection

May 12, 2026 · Protection

Whether you face physical danger, spiritual attack, or simply feel vulnerable, the psalms are filled with prayers for protection. Here are the psalms to pray when you need covering.

Psalm 91

“He who dwells in the shelter of the Most High will rest in the shadow of the Almighty.”Psalm 91:1

The ultimate protection psalm. Pray it over your home, your family, and your travels.

Psalm 121

“The Lord watches over you — the Lord is your shade at your right hand.”Psalm 121:5

A prayer for journeying — whether traveling or walking through a dangerous season.

Psalm 18

“The Lord is my rock, my fortress and my deliverer.”Psalm 18:2

David's song of deliverance from all his enemies. Bold, confident, and powerful.

Psalm 140

“Keep me safe, Lord, from the hands of the wicked.”Psalm 140:4

A prayer when you feel surrounded by evil intent. Honest and direct.
