Psalms for Strength & Courage

May 12, 2026 · Strength

When you feel weak, tired, or overwhelmed, these psalms will renew your strength and remind you that God fights for you.

Psalm 27

“Wait for the Lord; be strong and take heart and wait for the Lord.”Psalm 27:14

Psalm 138:3

“When I called, you answered me; you greatly emboldened me.”Psalm 138:3

Psalm 18:32-34

“It is God who arms me with strength and keeps my way secure.”Psalm 18:32

David's battle psalm — training your hands for war and your arms for battle.

Psalm 31:24

“Be strong and take heart, all you who hope in the Lord.”Psalm 31:24
