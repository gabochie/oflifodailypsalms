Psalms for Gratitude & Thanksgiving

May 12, 2026 · Gratitude

A thankful heart changes everything. When you want to express gratitude to God, the psalms give you the language.

Psalm 100

“Enter his gates with thanksgiving and his courts with praise.”Psalm 100:4

A short, joyful psalm perfect for starting your day with gratitude.

Psalm 103

“Bless the Lord, O my soul, and forget not all his benefits.”Psalm 103:2

A beautiful meditation on God's goodness, forgiveness, and love.

Psalm 136

“Give thanks to the Lord, for he is good. His love endures forever.”Psalm 136:1

A responsive psalm where every verse is answered with God's enduring love.
