Psalms for Grief & Loss

May 12, 2026 · Grief

Grief is a journey no one walks alone in scripture. The psalms give you permission to lament — to cry out, to question, and still hope.

Psalm 34:18

“The Lord is near to the brokenhearted and saves those who are crushed in spirit.”Psalm 34:18

Psalm 42

“Why, my soul, are you downcast? Put your hope in God.”Psalm 42:5

A psalm for when grief feels like a flood — honest about the pain, yet choosing hope.

Psalm 73

“Whom have I in heaven but you? And earth has nothing I desire besides you.”Psalm 73:25

When loss reorients your perspective, this psalm helps you see what truly matters.

Psalm 147:3

“He heals the brokenhearted and binds up their wounds.”Psalm 147:3
